About Me

I was born and raised in Brazil, where I initially pursued studies in engineering. However, I made a conscious decision to pivot my career towards the dynamic realm of Information Technology in my 30s.

As a native Portuguese speaker, I embarked on a journey to Australia in 2019, both to enhance my English proficiency and to immerse myself in a diverse global community. I have been living my best life, cherishing the opportunities this experience has brought.

My passion for technology led me to independently study and explore the IT landscape. This pursuit eventually culminated in my enrollment in a Diploma in IT, which I completed in April 2023.

Transitioning from my role as a Customer Relationship Officer, where I focused on bridging the gap between businesses and customers, I have recently embraced a new challenge as a Junior Test Analyst.In this capacity, I am dedicated to ensuring the quality and functionality of software products, conducting various tests and collaborating closely with development teams.

I am an ardent self-learner, constantly augmenting my knowledge and skills. In my free time, I relish spending quality moments with family and friends, driven by the aspiration to develop competencies that contribute to making the world a better place to live in.

Lastly, I possess a foundation in key IT technologies, including C++, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Office 365, and hold expertise in Customer Service, Sales, SEO, and Digital Marketing.

Moreover, I prioritize the nurturing of emotional intelligence, ensuring I remain adaptable and committed to meeting deadlines, whether working independently or as an integral part of a team.
